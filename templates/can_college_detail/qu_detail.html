📘 About Queen's University

Queen’s University, located in Kingston, Ontario, is a prestigious and historic Canadian institution blending tradition with modern academic innovation. With its elegant limestone campus set beside Lake Ontario, Queen’s offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs across disciplines such as arts, engineering, business, health sciences, and law. The student community is close-knit and vibrant, housing many active student societies, varsity teams, performing arts groups, and volunteer initiatives that enrich both university life and local life. Residential colleges foster strong bonds, while academic support, career services, wellness programs, and peer mentoring help students grow personally and professionally. Queen’s campus life is characterized by its friendly atmosphere, spirited school traditions, and a balance of intellectual engagement and social involvement in a beautiful lakeside setting.
