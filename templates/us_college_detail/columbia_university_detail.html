📘 About Colombia University

Columbia University, founded in 1754, is one of the oldest and most prestigious Ivy League institutions in the United States, renowned for its academic excellence, groundbreaking research, and global influence. Located in New York City’s Morningside Heights, Columbia offers a wide range of programs across the humanities, sciences, engineering, business, law, and medicine. The university’s distinctive Core Curriculum fosters critical thinking and interdisciplinary learning, while its strong faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant intellectual community attract top students from around the world. With unparalleled access to New York’s cultural, professional, and research opportunities, Columbia provides a dynamic environment that blends rigorous academics with a truly global perspective.

🏫 Campus Life

Campus life at Columbia University is dynamic, diverse, and deeply enriched by its location in the heart of New York City. The university’s Morningside Heights campus offers a vibrant academic and social environment, blending historic architecture with modern facilities, libraries, and collaborative spaces. Students can participate in over 500 clubs and organizations, ranging from cultural and academic groups to arts, sports, and community service. Columbia’s unique Core Curriculum fosters intellectual engagement, while its location provides unparalleled access to internships, cultural events, and networking opportunities in one of the world’s most influential cities. Balancing rigorous academics with a lively community, Columbia offers an exciting and globally connected student experience.
